FEMALE HEALTH SCREENING

Age 13-17

  • HPV vaccine
  • BP screen
  • Cholesterol screen
  • Health education on smoking, drinking, vaping, mental health, breast self examination

 

Age 20’s and 30’s

  • Annual GP Visit
  • Annual BP screen
  • Cervical cancer screening  every 3 years
  • <25 and sexually active : annual STI screen
  • >25 with new/multiple sexual partners: annual STI Screen
  • Genetic testing if 1st degree relative with cancers in the following regions
 breast ,ovary, cervix,colon


Age 40’s and 50’s

  • Annual GP visit
  • Annual routine blood tests including cholesterol
  • PAP TEST & Pelvic exam
